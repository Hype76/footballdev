## App name

Football Player Coach

## Subtitle or short description

Matchday and player tools for football coaches.

## Full description

Football Player Coach gives club staff quick access to the football workflows they need on a phone.

Coaches can open matchday, update live scores, record goals, correct scores, review players, save quick assessments, and see assigned sessions. The app is designed for active coaching situations where staff need large touch targets, simple choices, and fast updates.

The app works with an existing Football Player club workspace. Login is required and accounts are managed by the club through the web platform.

Payments, checkout, subscription management, bulk email, and club billing controls are not available in this app.

No gambling, no user generated public social feed, no public chat, no mature content.

## Reviewer notes

This app is for authorised football club staff. Login is required.

Use the supplied test coach account. The test account is connected to a test club workspace with players, teams, matchday data, sessions, and assessment fields.

Payments are handled outside the mobile app and are not available in this app. Subscription management remains on the desktop web platform.

Push notifications require a real iOS or Android device. Biometric unlock appears only when the device supports enrolled biometric security.

This review build uses the test database.

## Data use notes

The app may process:

- Account email and staff profile details.
- Club, team, player, session, and matchday records.
- Player assessment entries created by authorised staff.
- Device push notification token when notifications are enabled.

The app does not include third-party advertising or in-app purchases.
