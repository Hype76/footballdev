# Football Player Parents Store Metadata

Use this as the starting point for App Store Connect and Google Play Console.

## App name

Football Player Parents

## Subtitle or short description

Matchday updates and club messages for parents.

## Full description

Football Player Parents helps parents and guardians stay connected with their child's football club updates.

Parents can see linked child context, follow matchday updates, receive goal and full time alerts, read club messages, answer parent polls, and volunteer as a match scorer where the club allows it.

The app works with an existing Football Player parent portal account. Login is required and parent access must be linked by the club.

Payments, checkout, subscription management, club admin tools, staff workflows, and bulk email controls are not available in this app.

## Age rating notes

No gambling, no user generated public social feed, no public chat, no mature content.

The app may display linked child names, team details, match information, club messages, and parent poll content shared by the club.

## Reviewer notes

This app is for parents and guardians linked to a football club workspace. Login is required.

Use the supplied test parent account. The test account is connected to at least one linked child with matchday data, club messages, and an open parent poll.

Payments are handled outside the mobile app and are not available in this app. Subscription management remains on the desktop web platform.

Push notifications require a real iOS or Android device. Biometric unlock appears only when the device supports enrolled biometric security.

This review build uses the test database.

## Data use notes

The app may process:

- Account email and parent portal access details.
- Linked child, club, team, matchday, message, and poll records.
- Parent poll answers and scorer volunteer actions.
- Device push notification token when notifications are enabled.

The app does not include third-party advertising or in-app purchases.
