# Store Review Notes

Player Feedback Mobile is a free login-only companion app for existing club users.

The app does not include:

- Stripe checkout
- Subscription purchase screens
- Billing management
- Pricing pages
- Upgrade prompts
- External payment links

Access is controlled by the existing account and club permissions returned by Supabase. Accounts without current access see a neutral access state.
